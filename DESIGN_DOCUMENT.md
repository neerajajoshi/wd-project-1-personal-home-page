## Design Document for Catherine Han's Personal Home Page Project

# Project Description

This is a project for my Summer 2026 Web Development course at Northeastern University. It is a personal home page project to be developed as a static site using only vanilla HTML, CSS, and JS.

# User Personas

1. Other students in the course. They may be curious about the work of other students.
2. Recruiters. They may be interested in looking at the coding projects an applicant has done.

# User Stories

1. Another student finishes their project, and wonders what other students have built. They look at some other student's home pages, and are interested in looking at the actual site, in addition to learning more about the student's background.
2. A recruiter clicks on the site as they found it on the applicant's github. They read the information on the site, and gain a better understanding of the applicant's background.
